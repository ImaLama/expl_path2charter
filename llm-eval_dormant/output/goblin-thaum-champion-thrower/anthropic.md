# Griknak "The Holy Hurler" — Level 8 Goblin Thaumaturge

## Build Concept

A goblin who discovered a battered holy symbol of Cayden Cailean in a trash heap and became convinced the god of bravery chose *him* specifically. He combines the Thaumaturge's Exploit Vulnerability with blessed throwing weapons, using the **Weapon Implement** for damage enhancement and the **Amulet Implement** for survivability — critical for a small, fragile goblin who needs to stay in the fight long enough to keep throwing.

---

## Implement Choices Explained

**First Implement (Level 1): Weapon Implement**
The Weapon Implement is the cornerstone. When you select a weapon as your implement, it gains the **Implement's Empowerment** bonus damage (+2 at level 1, scaling to +4 at level 5+, +6 at level 9+) on Strikes. Critically, the Thaumaturge's **Exploit Vulnerability** applies to Strikes with your weapon implement regardless of range. Choosing a returning throwing weapon as your weapon implement means every thrown Strike benefits from both Exploit Vulnerability's bonus damage AND Implement's Empowerment. The Weapon Implement's adept benefit (gained at level 5 when you get your second implement) lets you shift damage types — excellent for overcoming resistances that Exploit Vulnerability has identified.

**Second Implement (Level 5): Amulet Implement**
The Amulet grants the **Amulet's Abeyance** reaction: when you or an ally within 15 feet takes damage, you grant resistance equal to 2 + your level (so resistance 10 at level 8). This is a reaction you can use every round. For a thrown weapon build that operates at some distance from enemies but still within 15 feet of allies, this is superb — it keeps you and your party alive. The Amulet's Intensify Vulnerability benefit also adds a status penalty to the target's damage against you and allies, stacking beautifully with the defensive theme. This shores up the goblin's low HP and keeps the party standing.

*Why not Lantern or Tome?* Lantern is great but its benefits are more exploration-focused. Tome requires being in melee to use its reaction effectively. The Amulet's consistent damage mitigation complements a ranged attacker who still needs to be within 30 feet for Exploit Vulnerability's personal antithesis damage.
