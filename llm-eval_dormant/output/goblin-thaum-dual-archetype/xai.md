### Personality & Backstory
**“Scrapshield” (Kib the Unburnt)** is a wiry, soot-stained charhide goblin who grew up in the scrapyards of Absalom. He believes every broken thing has a spirit that can be woken up if you hit it hard enough with the right object. After he accidentally awakened an old holy sword and a shield that once belonged to a fallen Redeemer of Sarenrae, the sword and shield began arguing in his head about morality. Kib decided the best way to shut them up was to become a hero — mostly so they’d stop yelling at him.

He now travels with a ridiculous scrap-and-bone shield strapped to his arm and a jagged dogslicer that occasionally glows with divine fire. He’s loud, opportunistic, surprisingly loyal, and has an unhealthy obsession with “fixing” magic items by hitting them with other magic items.

### Rule Notes & GM Adjudication Flags
1. **Exemplar Dedication at level 6** is legal (character level 6). Confirm your GM allows *Rage of Elements* content.
2. **Shield Ally** (Thaumaturge) was delayed to level 5 because level 4 was used for the Champion archetype feat. This is standard and legal.
3. **Deific Weapon** requires a deity. We chose Sarenrae to match the Redeemer cause and the “holy sword” flavor.
4. **Implement’s Empowerment** + **Intense Implement** + **Exploit Vulnerability** + **Deific Weapon** creates very strong single-target damage for a 3/4 caster chassis.
5. Charhide heritage + Toughness + high Con gives respectable durability for a goblin.

This build is 100% RAW compliant with all listed books (CRB, APG, TV, RoE). All dedication taxes and level requirements have been respected.
