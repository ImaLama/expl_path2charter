## Archetype feat tax
PF2e multiclass dedication rule:
- after taking a dedication feat, you **cannot take another dedication feat** until you have taken **two other feats from that archetype**, unless a rule says otherwise.

This does **not** force you to take two more archetype feats immediately. It only restricts taking another **dedication** feat.

Since this build only uses **Champion Dedication** and does not plan to enter another archetype, we are fine.

### Legal feat timing
- Level 2 class feat: **Champion Dedication**
- Level 4 class feat: **Basic Devotion**
- Level 6 class feat: **Champion’s Reaction** via Basic Devotion is a common target depending on cause and feat access, but feat naming/access depends on the exact Champion archetype wording version.
- Level 8 class feat: another legal Champion archetype feat, if available and desired

However, archetype feat access is constrained by:
- feat level
- whether the feat is available to archetypes
- whether it requires class features you don’t have

To avoid any risk of illegal feat selection, I’ll use a conservative, clearly legal package.
