Here is a complete, highly optimized Level 8 Goblin Thaumaturge build featuring the Champion Dedication. 

This build transforms your goblin into a battlefield-controlling skirmisher who uses thrown weapons and spatial magic to punish enemies from afar while keeping allies safe.

### **1. Ancestry Breakdown**
*   **Ancestry:** Goblin
*   **Heritage:** Unbreakable Goblin (Grants 10 starting HP instead of 6, helping offset your lower Constitution early on).
*   **Level 1 Ancestry Feat:** **Goblin Scuttle**. (Reaction: When an ally ends a move adjacent to you, you can Step. This is excellent for maintaining your preferred throwing distance and staying out of melee).
*   **Level 5 Ancestry Feat:** **Skittering Scuttle**. (Upgrades Goblin Scuttle so you can Stride up to half your Speed instead of Stepping. Incredible mobility for a ranged character).

### **2. Background**
*   **Background:** Guard (Fits the Paladin cause perfectly).
*   **Skill Training:** Intimidation, Warfare Lore.
*   **Background Feat:** **Quick Coercion**.

### **3. Class & Implements**
*   **Class:** Thaumaturge (*Dark Archive*)
*   **Key Ability:** Charisma
*   **First Implement (Level 1): Weapon Implement.** 
    *   *Weapon Choice:* **Chakram** (Martial, 1d8 Slashing, Thrown 20 ft). Thaumaturges have martial weapon proficiency, making the Chakram the highest-damage 1-handed thrown weapon available.
    *   *Adept Upgrade (Level 7):* You upgrade your Weapon Implement to Adept. This gives you a permanent **+2 status bonus to damage rolls** with your Chakram and unlocks its Critical Specialization (Dart group: target takes 1d6 persistent bleed damage, plus an item bonus equal to your weapon's potency rune).
*   **Second Implement (Level 5): Mirror Implement.** 
    *   *Synergy:* The Mirror is arguably the best implement for a ranged/thrown build. For 1 action, you project a reflection of yourself up to 15 feet away. You count as occupying *both* spaces for attacks, line of sight, and flanking. You can throw your Chakram from your reflection's space to effectively increase your range, attack around corners, ignore cover, or safely project your Champion aura into the frontline without risking your actual body!

### **4. Ability Score Progression**
To hit accurately with thrown weapons, you need high Dexterity. For damage and Champion prerequisites, you need Strength. For your Thaumaturge abilities, Charisma is paramount.
*   **Starting Array (Level 1):** 
    *   *Base:* Str 10, Dex 10, Con 10, Int 10, Wis 10, Cha 10
    *   *Goblin:* Dex, Cha, Free (Str), Flaw (Wis)
    *   *Guard:* Str, Cha
    *   *Class:* Cha
    *   *Free Boosts:* Str, Dex, Con, Cha
    *   *Level 1 Stats:* **Str 14 (+2), Dex 16 (+3), Con 12 (+1), Int 10 (+0), Wis 8 (-1), Cha 18 (+4)**. *(Str 14 and Cha 14 legally satisfy the Champion Dedication prerequisites).*
*   **Level 5 Boosts:** Str, Dex, Con, Cha
*   **Final Level 8 Stats:** **Str 16 (+3), Dex 18 (+4), Con 14 (+2), Int 10 (+0), Wis 8 (-1), Cha 19 (+4)**.

### **5. Skill Proficiency Optimization**
*   **Esoteric Lore (Cha):** Trained at 1. *Thaumaturge automatically upgrades this to Expert at 3 and Master at 7.* (Current modifier: +18).
*   **Medicine (Wis):** Trained at 1, Expert at 3, Master at 7. (Vital for keeping your team topped up between fights).
*   **Intimidation (Cha):** Trained at 1, Expert at 5. (Demoralize is a great third action).
*   **Other Trained Skills (Level 1):** Athletics, Acrobatics, Stealth, Thievery.

### **6. Complete Feat Selection**
*   **Level 1 (Class): Scroll Thaumaturgy.** Allows you to draw and activate scrolls with the hand holding your implement. Great utility.
*   **Level 2 (Class / Archetype): Champion Dedication.** You must choose the **Paladin** cause (requires Lawful Good alignment) and a deity (e.g., Iomedae). This grants you training in heavy armor and the Champion's code.
*   **Level 2 (Skill): Battle Medicine.** Mid-combat healing without magic.
*   **Level 3 (General): Fleet.** +5 feet to Speed. Crucial for a skirmisher.
*   **Level 4 (Class): Breached Defenses.** When you Exploit Vulnerability, you can tailor your Chakram to bypass complex resistances (like Cold Iron or Silver).
*   **Level 4 (Skill): Ward Medic.** Heal multiple allies during 10-minute breaks.
*   **Level 5 (Ancestry): Skittering Scuttle.** *(See Ancestry).*
*   **Level 6 (Class / Archetype): Champion's Reaction.** Grants you the Paladin's **Retributive Strike**. When an enemy within 15 feet hits your ally, you reduce the damage and make a melee Strike against the enemy.
*   **Level 6 (Skill): Continual Recovery.** Reduces Medicine cooldown to 10 minutes.
*   **Level 7 (General): Toughness.** +8 max HP.
*   **Level 8 (Class / Archetype): Advanced Devotion -> Ranged Reprisal.** *This is the linchpin of the build.* It explicitly allows you to use your Retributive Strike with a ranged weapon. Because a thrown weapon is a ranged weapon when thrown, you can now reduce damage to an ally and hurl your Chakram at the attacker as a reaction!
*   **Level 8 (Skill): Intimidating Prowess.** Gives a circumstance bonus to Intimidation based on your Strength.

### **8. Tactical Combat Synergy**
Your typical combat flow is highly dynamic. On turn one, use **Exploit Vulnerability** (1A) to target a weakness, then use **Mirror's Reflection** (1A) to project your clone 15 feet away into a flanking position or near your frontline allies. Finally, **Strike** (1A) by throwing your Chakram from either your real body or the reflection. 
On the enemy's turn, because you occupy both spaces, you project a massive 15-foot aura of protection around *both* bodies. If an enemy attacks an ally within 15 feet of either of you, you use **Retributive Strike** (modified by **Ranged Reprisal**) to reduce the damage to your ally and immediately hurl your Returning Chakram at the offender as a reaction!

### **9. Personality & Backstory**
**Gribx "The Law"** is not your typical goblin. Found as an infant by a patrol of Iomedaean guards and raised in a city barracks, Gribx became obsessed with the concepts of justice, shielding the weak, and polished brass. Though too small to wield a traditional longsword or wear massive plate mail effectively, he discovered a natural talent for hurling the chakram—a weapon he treats as a spinning halo of divine retribution. Armed with an ancient hand-mirror he believes reflects the "truth of the soul" (his Thaumaturge implement) and sworn to the Paladin's code, Gribx patrols the battlefield. He is loud, rigidly dutiful, and fiercely protective of his friends, using his bizarre spatial magic to be in two places at once so he can lob spinning blades at anyone who dares strike his allies.
